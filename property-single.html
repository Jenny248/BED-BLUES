HOLIDAYS & STAY IN STYLE

Our holiday home “Bed 'n Blues” is a luxurious accommodation for 2 or 4 people, located on the ground floor in the city center of Halen. It includes a very nice, pleasant living room with radio, TV, DVD player and WIFI. There is its own kitchen with dishwasher, fridge with freezer, hob, microwave, coffee maker, kettle, toaster, oven, cooking utensils, etc ... The bedrooms are very nicely furnished with luxurious box springs and the bathroom has a spacious shower and a sink with storage cupboard. .

Bath towels, hairdryer and basic products are provided. The toilet is in a separate room with a small sink and mirror. There is a large terrace, accessible from the kitchen, with table, chairs, BBQ and then a fairly large garden.
